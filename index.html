The One Where We Found Home

Not in a place.
In a show.
In a couch.
In each other.

You’d say, “Just one episode.”

I’d say, “Okay, last one.”

And five minutes later… I’d be asleep.

Zzz...

The One Where I Always Slept Early

But I never slept through the part that mattered.

You.

I don’t miss the show.

I miss your voice reacting to scenes.
Your laugh between dialogues.
The way comfort felt effortless.

I’m not asking for anything.

Those nights were my safe place.

Even if I slept early…
I was exactly where I wanted to be.
